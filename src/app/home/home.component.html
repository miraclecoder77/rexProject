Subscription Plans

Merchants are eligible to at least one plan per time which can be activated at any point on the RexRetail platform. The subscription plan can be monthly,
half-yearly, or annually depending on the preference or convenience of the merchant.

Each merchant is entitled to a trial period of 10 days. Over this period, the merchant will not be requested to pay any value for the subscription.
