Want to renew your subscription plan?

To pay for your subscription renewal, you'll need your POS. This quick guide will help you through the payment process.

  • Login to your RexRetail account on your POS Device.
  • Navigate to the Subscription Page.
  • Select desired subscription plan.
  • Insert your debit card.
  • Enter your debit card pin.
  • Click on the "Pay Now" button.
